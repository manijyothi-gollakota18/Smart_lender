Loan Approvals by Credit History

A credit history status of 1.0 (Good) is the strongest positive predictor of loan approval. Applicants with bad or no credit history (0.0) are almost universally declined.

Applicant Income Distribution

Most applicants have monthly incomes concentrated below $10,000, with a long right tail of high-earning individuals requesting larger loans.

Average Loan Amount by Education & Status

Graduates on average request and receive larger loans compared to non-graduates. Approved loans (Y) tend to have slightly lower or comparable averages to declined ones within groups, indicating strict debt-to-income checks.
